I am honored to have received the **Best Oral Presentation Award** at the 1th International Conference on Frontiers of Energy and Environment Engineering (CFEEE 2022).

This presentation discussed the methodologies for data generation method for power system operation considering geographical correlations and actual operation characteristics. This work was conducted as part of my research in power system and later published in *Energy Reports*.
